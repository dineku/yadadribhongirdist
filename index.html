Specialties of Yadadri Bhuvanagiri

Yadadri Bhuvanagiri is known for its unique blend of **religious tourism**, **agriculture**, and **handicrafts**.

  • Religious Tourism: Home to the **Yadadri Temple** and **Bhongir Fort**, the district is a significant religious and historical destination.
  • Agriculture: Known for the cultivation of **paddy, cotton**, and **turmeric**.
  • Handicrafts: Famous for **Pochampally Ikat** and handloom weaving industries.
